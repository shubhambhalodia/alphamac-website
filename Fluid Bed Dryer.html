Application & Process:

The AlphaMac Laboratory Scale Fluid Bed Dryer model is used for small batch size production or R&D purpose. Fluid bed technology is a offer an effective and efficient method for drying and agglomerating. The Lab Fluid Bed Dryer has universal application for drying of powder and granules.. The Fluid Bed system have a bed of solid particles which are fluidized by passing a stream of warm air upward through a specially designed perforated sheet. The upward velocity of air is so maintained so as to slightly lift the solid particles and set them in motion. This motion can be utilized to bring about mixing as well as forward movement of the solids particles. The air is heated and the process hot air evaporates the fluid and dries the solids. Fines get agglomerated to larger granules particles thus providing large size.
