Welcome to AlphaMac Pharma Machinery

Application & Process:

The AlphaMac Laboratory Scale Extruder for small batch size production or R&D purpose. The equipment has two major parts one is the die screen of required size and other is pressing screw, needles coming out of the extruder are cut into definite length by cutter blade. The size of sieve can very from 0.5 to 5 mm hole size. For conversion into spherical spheres/pellet a Spher’oidizer needs to be used conjunction with the extruder.

Design Features:

• Design is cGMP Practices compliance with contact parts AISI 316 / 316L.

• Easy to install, operate, clean, convenient & user friendly.

• Flat type pressing blade (Axial Pressing Type)

• The assembly is closed by tri clove clamps for easy in operation.

• Output: 500 gram to 5 kg per hours.

• Sieves available size is 0.5, 0.8,1,1.5, 2, 2.5, 3, 4 & 5mm

• Product contact parts are mirror finished and non contact parts are matt finished.
