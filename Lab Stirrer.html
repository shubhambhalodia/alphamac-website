Application & Process:

The Life Laboratory Scale Stirrer / Agitator / Homogenizer for small batch size production or R&D purpose. It is applicable to blending, dispersing, homogenization, mixing of liquid, ointment, solution, viscous material and solid-liquid. Stirrer / Agitator/ Homogenizer mix two or more Liquids to Required Results. It consists of two parts 1) Drive 2) Impeller. This is direct drive stirrer with homogenizer blade.

Design Features:

• Design is cGMP Practices compliance with contact parts AISI 316 / 316L.

• Easy to install, operate, clean, convenient & user friendly.

• Capacity available for volume of 2 liter to 25 liter for lab and Pilot scale.

• This intermittent type high-shearing homogenization emulsion machine is high-powered stirring system.

• Optional feature for different type of Blade like (1) Propeller ( 2) Slotted (3)Homogenizer.

• The stirrer assembly is mounted on Telescopic stand.
